环状的

###
From Latin ānulāris (“ring-shaped”); (possibly through French annulaire), from ānulus (“ring”).


###
- Pertaining to, or having the form of, a ring; ring-shaped; in the shape of an [[annulus]]. 
	e.g. annular fiber
- Banded or marked with circles.

###
Synonyms:
ring-shaped
