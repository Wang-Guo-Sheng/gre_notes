###
From [[lime]] + [[stone]].

###
Noun
- An abundant rock of marine and fresh-water [[sediments]]; primarily composed of [[calcite]] (CaCO3); it occurs in a variety of forms, both crystalline and amorphous. 
- (attributive) Pertaining to or made of limestone
